# Write half hour daily

## Things to write
- Cover letters
- Journal
- Develop ideas
- Develop stories
- Reviews of movies or books
- Plans for a game.
- Plans for projects

### when

After coffee, lunch or dinner

## Journal
- What has happened
- How I feel
- How do I feel about things
- Develop things
- Impersonal things, so not concerned to get feedback

### when

- After have an idea
- After experiencing something, maybe as simple as watching something.
- Morning and or evening

## Get feedback
- Personal writing
- Impersonal writing
- From a large language model

### when

After each draft

## Spelling
- Wordlist
- Testing
  - Have a person help
  - Use text to speech (TTP) of phone or other accessability features
  - Find a spelling testing application
  - Make a spelling testing application

### when

Weekly
